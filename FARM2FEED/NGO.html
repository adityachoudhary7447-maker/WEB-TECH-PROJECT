Our Journey & Mission

psychology

The Seed is Sown (2018)

Farm2Feed was founded by a group of agriculturists and social workers who witnessed the widening gap between organic farm production and urban demand, while hunger persisted in rural corners.

handshake

Empowering the Roots (2020)

We established a direct-to-farmer model, eliminating exploitative middlemen. This ensured 40% higher income for 2,000+ small-scale farmers across Maharashtra and Karnataka.

verified

Global Standards (2022)

Achieved ISO and NPOP Organic certifications, opening doors for our farmers to global markets and ensuring that every product on your table is pure, safe, and traceable.

Feeding the Future

For every basket of organic produce you purchase, we donate a portion of the harvest to our NGO partners. This "Community Harvest" model has helped serve over half a million meals to children in underserved regions.
